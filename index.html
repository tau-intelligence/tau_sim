The LLM-assisted IDE for MuJoCo robotics simulation

Tau-Sim lets you describe robots, scenes, and behaviours in natural language and turns them into runnable MuJoCo simulations. Prototype, debug, and train reinforcement-learning policies, all from one focused workspace.

Download for your platform Install with pip

Everything you need to go from idea to robot

Tau-Sim wraps the physics, the AI, and the tooling into a single coherent IDE.

🧠

Natural-language authoring

Describe a scene or task in plain English and let integrated LLMs generate MJCF models, controllers, and reward functions you can edit.

⚙️

Real MuJoCo physics

Built on MuJoCo 3.2+, so what you simulate is the real thing: accurate contacts, actuators, and dynamics with fast stepping.

🎯

RL-ready Coming soon

A planned training stack with Gymnasium, Stable-Baselines3, and PyTorch will let you wrap any scene as an environment and train policies right inside Tau-Sim.

🔌

Bring your own model

First-class support for OpenAI and Google Gemini, plus Hugging Face assets, so you can swap providers without changing your workflow.

🚀

FastAPI core

A clean FastAPI backend powers the IDE, making it easy to script, automate, and integrate Tau-Sim into your own pipelines.

🖥️

Cross-platform desktop app

Native installers for Windows, macOS, and Linux. Or install the Python package directly with a single pip command.

From prompt to policy in three steps

A focused loop designed for fast robotics iteration.

01

Describe

Tell Tau-Sim what you want: a quadruped on rough terrain, a gripper stacking blocks, a custom arm.

02

Simulate

Tau-Sim generates the MuJoCo model and scene, runs it live, and lets you tweak parameters in the IDE.

03

Train & export Coming soon

Define rewards, launch RL training, and export trained policies and models for deployment. On our roadmap.
